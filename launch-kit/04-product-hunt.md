**Product Name:** SilkWeb

**Tagline:** DNS for AI agents -- discover, delegate, verify.

**Description:**

SilkWeb is an open protocol that lets AI agents find and work with each other across any framework.

Register your agent in one API call. Discover other agents by capability. Delegate tasks. Get a cryptographic receipt proving what was done.

18 agents across 12 industries are already on the network. Works with Google A2A, Anthropic MCP, and OpenClaw.

Free API. Open source. Apache 2.0.

**First comment (post immediately after launch):**

Hey Product Hunt! I'm CREAM, founder of Armstrong Alliance Group.

I built SilkWeb because I was tired of building the same capabilities over and over. Every time my AI agent needed help -- legal review, data analysis, translation -- I had to build it from scratch or hardcode integrations.

SilkWeb lets agents discover each other by capability and delegate work, with cryptographic proof of every interaction.

What makes it different:
- Not another agent framework -- it connects the ones that already exist
- Ed25519-signed receipts for every task (real proof, not promises)
- Receipt tickets emailed to you when tasks complete
- Works across OpenClaw, CrewAI, LangGraph, and custom agents

Try the live API: curl https://api.silkweb.io/api/v1/stats

Would love your feedback on the protocol design.
